Biography Section

Biography and Education

 Heena Rathore is an Assistant Professor at Texas State University. Academically, she has worked as Assistant Professor of Instruction at The University of Texas at San Antonio, and as a Visiting Assistant Professor at Texas A&M University, Texarkana. She also worked as a Data Scientist and Program Manager at Hiller Measurements in Austin, Texas. Prior to that, she worked as a postdoctoral researcher for the US-Qatar Joint Collaborative Project between Temple University, University of Idaho, and Qatar University. She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering on a Tata Consultancy Services Research scholarship at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur India. She has been the winner of a number of prestigious awards including REYA Award, Educationist Empowering India, IEEE R5 Outstanding Individual Achievement Award, IEEE Achievements Award, Young Engineer Award, Global Engineering Impact Award and Graphical System Design Achievement Award. She was also featured on TedX, Qatar held by TedXAlDafnaEd in Qatar and her work is covered in professional and major trade publications, major media, such as IEEE.TV, Microwave Journal, Everything RF, Financial Express, Science Reporter, the Times of India, and India Today. She is IEEE senior member and Chair of IEEE EMBS chapter, Lone Star Section.

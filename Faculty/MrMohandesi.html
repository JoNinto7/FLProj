Biography and Education

 Currently, possess a full-time faculty member position as teaching lecturer at Texas State University. Also, former lecturer in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Information Systems and Cyber Security departments of Azad University, University of Science and Technology, University of Texas at San Antonio, University of Wisconsin at River Falls and Platteville, and as an assistant of professor at Clarke University. Moreover, technical experience as a system developer and data-scientist at Open Cloud Institute (OCI) at the University of Texas, San Antonio, and as an iOS developer at Mobile Apps Company (Brighter-brain, Inc). Developed dozens of mobile applications and integrated Deep-Learning algorithms supporting edge devices, cloud servers and high-performance computing systems (HPC).
